About

An inspiring headline about yourself.

Rajeev is a startup enthusiast and youth mentor passionate about building purposeful lives. He has founded two startups, with his first venture pitched to Shark Tank’s Ashneer Grover.

Blending entrepreneurial drive with a deep-rooted practice in yoga and meditation. Rajeev empowers young minds to lead balanced, mindful, and impactful lives. He has been practising Heartfulness meditation since 2011, bringing inner clarity into professional spaces. With hands-on experience across tech, innovation, and education, Rajeev inspires the next generation to align ambition with awareness.
